Eligibility Questionnaire

Answer 5 quick questions to see if you or your loved one may qualify for Michigan's Home Care Compensation Program.

Home Care Compensation — Eligibility Check

This takes about 2 minutes. Your answers are private and not stored.

Question 1 of 5

Do you or the person you're caring for currently have Medicaid?

Question 2 of 5

Are you a resident of the state of Michigan?

Question 3 of 5

Is the person receiving care currently living at home (not in a hospital or nursing facility)?

Question 4 of 5

Does the person receiving care have a medical condition, disability, or cognitive impairment?

Question 5 of 5

Does the person need help with any daily activities such as grooming, transportation, eating, or personal hygiene?

🎉

You May Qualify!

Based on your answers, you may be eligible for Michigan's Home Care Compensation Program. A PureLife specialist will help you complete the process at no cost to you.



💛

You May Not Qualify Right Now

Based on your answers, this specific program may not be the right fit — but there may be other services available to you. Please reach out and our team will explore all options with you.
